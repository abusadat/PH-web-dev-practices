How to Make Cake

Cake is a sweet baked dessert that is popular in many cultures.

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and vanilla extract, and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
